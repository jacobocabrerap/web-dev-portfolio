Colombian Arepas Recipe

Desciption

"Arepa is a Colombian dish made with flour and cheese as the main ingredients, it can be eaten for breakfast, it can go along with your lunch or even dinner with a nice hot coffee."

Ingredients

  • Pre-cooked Corn flour 1 3/4 cups
  • Shredded Cheese (Mozzarella) 125g
  • Sliced Cheese (Mozzarella) 125g
  • Queso Costeño (Optional) 70g
  • Water/Milk (One or the other) 1 1/2 cups
  • Butter 1 spoon
  • Sugar 1 spoon

Steps Arepas with Cheese filling

  1. Place the warm milk in a mixing bowl, add the sugar and dissolve. Then gradually add the flour. Stir first with a spoon, and then with your hands, until all of the cornmeal has been added.
  2. So, add the grated cheeses, the mozzarella and the costeño. Love very well until the mixture is homogeneous and moldable.
  3. Divide the dough into four equal-sized portions, and form into small balls. Then take the first ball in your hands, and sink it in the center, as if you wanted to make a bowl of dough. Place a small piece of cheese in the center.
  4. Then, join the edges at the top, sealing the arepa.
  5. Delicately using your fingertips, perfect any detail and make sure the arepa is even.
  6. Melt the butter in the pan where the arepas will be made and spread the butter over the entire surface. Then, place the arepas to cook, about five minutes on each side. Remove from pan and consume immediately. If you wish you can place an extra slice of cheese on top.
